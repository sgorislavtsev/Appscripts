    ⚙️ Settings

    💡 Codes are single letters used in the schedule grid. Default codes are pre-loaded. You can add custom codes, edit labels/colors, or remove codes not used at your location. The L (Lunch) code cannot be removed — it's used for break tracking.
    KeyLabel / NameColorTypeActions
    ⚡ These defaults control how the Auto-Scheduler fills shifts. They apply to every run unless overridden in the wizard. Changes take effect immediately and are saved with your preferences.
    ⏱️ Shift Shape
    Longest a single shift can run, not counting lunch. Prevents 10+ hour slogs.
    hrs max
    Skip a day rather than create a shift shorter than this. Avoids 1h micro-shifts.
    hrs min
    Hard daily ceiling — includes any already-scheduled time. Protects against overtime on any single day.
    hrs/day
    Preferred start/end when a person has no profile availability window set. Leave blank to use the grid hours.

    📅 Weekly Structure
    Stop scheduling someone once they hit this many days in the week, even if hours remain.
    days max
    Try to give everyone at least this many shifts — useful for part-timers who need guaranteed days.
    days min
    Force at least one day off after this many back-to-back days. Protects against 7-day stretches.
    days in a row
    How to spread someone's weekly hours across their available days.
